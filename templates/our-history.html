Our History

Keeping our church running smoothly

The Meledath 'Family Directory' is now seeing the light of day. Although efforts have been made for this historical record over many periods, we are very happy that our eternal dream and that of our ancestors has been fulfilled. Thank God that the Family Association was formed in the above and the directory was published within three years.
Our family tree begins in the upper part of Chalakudy. Legend has it that a Namboothiri named Vasuden married a Christian woman from the 'Ullattikkulam' family tree in Chalakudy itself and later became a Christian. His descendants lived in Chalakudy and the surrounding area of Karukutty. One of these families from Karukutty reached Ollur and bought the land of the 'Pozhath' Nayans. Then the first priest of our family tree, Francis Kathanar, donated that family tree to the Ollur St. Mary's Math, and the remaining six brothers lived in houses from the Math to Panamkuttichirakulam. Later, families moved to places like Thrikkur, Marathakkara and Chittissery.
When our ancestral home, which has a great Christian tradition, was divided, a portion was given to Rome and in return, a letter of thanks was received from the Pope.
I would like to express the committee's gratitude to all those who have worked tirelessly to complete this directory, and request your forgiveness for any minor errors. I dedicate this directory to the feet of our venerable ancestors who have walked before us from the ancestral home above.
